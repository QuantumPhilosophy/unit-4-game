Unit 4 Game

You will be given a random number as a Target Score at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to Your Score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

  • You win the game when Your Score matches the Target Score.
  • You lose the game if Your Score goes above the Target Score.
